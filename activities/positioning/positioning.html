What is normal flow in HTML?

The normal flow is the way that block and inline elements are displayed on page before any changes are made to layout.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float places an elements on the left or right side its container allowing text and inline elements to wrap around it. We clear the float property to control the floating elements by preventing overlapping

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Relative is that the element is positioned relative to its normal position. Absolute is that the element is positioned absolutely to its first positioned parent. Fixed is that the element is positioned related to the browser window.
