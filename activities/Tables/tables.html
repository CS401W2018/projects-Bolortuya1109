Answers of Questions

When do you use a table?

We can present data in rows and columns for schedules and comparisons. Price lists and test results are compared.

When do you not use a table?

Lists and articles is not structured in tabular form.

When do you need to do extra work for table accessibility?

The table contains complex structures such as multiple header rows or merged cells. It includes interactive elements such as buttons, links, forms inside cells.
