Graphics

What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

JPEG is best for photographs and images with many colors and gradients. It provides good compression while maintaining quality for realistic images.

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

GIF is ideal for simple images with few colors, animations, and graphics with transparency.

What is unique about the PNG format?

PNG supports lossless compression and transparency, making it ideal for high-quality images and graphics with transparent backgrounds.

How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?

You can resize your photo using image editing software such as Paint before uploading it to your website

Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?

Resizing photos with editing software reduces file size, improves loading speed, and ensures better quality than relying on CSS scaling.
