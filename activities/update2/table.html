Types of Mental Health Support

This table provides information about various types of mental health support, their duration, and the progress typically seen over time.

Type of Mental Health Support Duration Progress
Therapy 6 months Improved mood, better coping skills
Self-Care 2 months Reduced anxiety, increased self-awareness
Support Groups Ongoing Stronger support system, reduced loneliness
