Questions:

The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

It specifies the URL where form data is sent when submitted.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

Method is to define how data is sent (get) or (post).

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

Get is to send data in the URL, less secure, limited data, used for searches. Post sends data in the request body, more secure, allows large data, used for forms like login/signup.
