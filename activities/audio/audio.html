What are the positives and negatives of hosting your videos on YouTube?

YouTube gives you access to a huge audience and allows you to make money from ads. It is free and easy to use. But YouTube takes a cut of your earnings, can remove your videos anytime, and it is hard to stand out because of so many videos.

What is the most useful thing you can do with a transform?

Transforms change things to make them more useful. In data, they help make data easier to analyze. In graphics, they move or resize objects. In machine learning, they improve data for better results.

How do you stay safe online?

Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication to protect your accounts. Be careful what personal info you share, and make sure websites are secure. Avoid clicking on suspicious links and keep your software updated. A VPN can help keep your activity private.
