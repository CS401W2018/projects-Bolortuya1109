What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to a person's emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how individuals think, feel, and behave in daily life, as well as how they handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions. Good mental health allows people to cope with life's challenges, work productively, and contribute to their communities.
