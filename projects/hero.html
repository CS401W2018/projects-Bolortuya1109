My Hero - Song Hye Kyo

Song Hye Kyo is my favorite Korean actress known for her exceptional performances in various hit dramas. Her elegance, talent, and versatility have made her one of the most beloved actress in the South Korea. She has timeless beauty and has worked as a model for top brands. She has won numerous awards.

Most Popular Actress - Blue Dragon Film Awards (2007)
Blue Dragon Film Awards is one of Korea's top film awards. This recognition highlighted her successful transition from TV dramas.

Grand Prize - KBS Drama Awards (2016)
The Grand Prize is the highest honor in Korean entertainment awards. Her performances as Kang Mo in Descendants of the Sun was widely praised, and drama became a global hit.
