Structural Tags

What goes in the header?

The header is used to contain introductory content or navigattional links. It includes website logo, website title, a primary navigation bar, and introductory information.

What goes in the Nav? The nav is used for primary navigation, containing links to major sections or pages of the site. For example, navigation menus, breadcrumbs and links like Home, About and Contact so on.
Can the Nav go in the header? Yes, the nav can go inside the header.
Does it have to go in the header?
Does the Nav contain links to another website? The nav is used for internal navigation within the same website.

What goes in Main?

The main is the central container for the unique content of page such as the main artivle or content of the page.

What goes in the Footer?

The footer contains information at the bottom of the page. For example, contact information, and copyright notices.
